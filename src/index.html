Drones 101

Welcome to the Drone 101 course!

This course is an introductions to drones and all that they are capable of. In the middle of this page you will see some block codes. This is where you will find different blocks that will control your drone to the right. As you learn more about drones you will have challenges that reflect what drones can do in real life. Complete the challenges to move on to the next lesson.

The following course topics highlight what industries are using drones:

  • Agricultrue: Farmers use drones to monitor crop health, assess irrigation needs, and apply pesticides or fertilizers with greater precision, a practice known as precision agriculture. The data collected helps optimize yields and reduce resource waste.

In this application, you will use Blockly, a visual programming language, to create sequences of commands for a simulated drone. Watch the simulation to see your code in action! Feel free to play around with coding the drone and see what it is capable of.
